How adoption works

1

Browse our animals

Browse the cats and dogs in our care and find the friend who feels right for you.

2

Apply online

Fill in a short application telling us about you, your home and your lifestyle.

3

Chat & home check

4

Welcome them home

We handle the travel and paperwork — you get ready to welcome your new family member.

5

Aftercare

Once your furry friend is all settled in to their forever home, love them and show them what its like to be loved!

Our story

A second chance 1,400 miles from home

MM Animal Rescue began with one simple belief — that every animal, no matter where they’re born, deserves love, safety and a family. What started as a few volunteers feeding strays in Romania is now a lifeline carrying cats and dogs to forever homes across the UK.

❤

Compassion first

Every decision we make puts the wellbeing of the animal at its heart, always.

⚑

Total transparency

We’re 100% volunteer-run, so every donation goes straight to vet care, food and travel.

⌂

Lifelong support

Adoption isn’t the end of the journey. We’re here for you and your pet, for life.
