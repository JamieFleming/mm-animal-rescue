OUR MISSION

At MM Animal Rescue, we are dedicated to rescuing and rehoming cats and dogs from Romania to the UK. Our team of passionate animal lovers works tirelessly to provide these animals with the love and care they deserve. We believe that every animal deserves a second chance at life, and we are committed to making that happen.

Join us in our mission to save lives and give these animals the loving homes they deserve.
